A Few Words on Creativity

What is it and why is it important to game develepment? Let's explore the quotes below, before we look at this video.

1:19 from Damonpackwood on Vimeo

CHARLES MINGUS


"Creativity is more than just being different. Anybody can play weird; thats easy. What is hard is to be as simple as Bach. Making the simple, awesomely simple, thats creativity."

GEORGE BERNARD SHAW



"Imagination is the beginning of creation. You imagine what you desire, you will what you imagine, and at last, you create what you will."

STEVE JOBS


"Creativity is just connecting things. When you ask creative people how they did something, they feel a little guilty because they didnt really do it, they just saw something. It seemed obvious to them after a while."

CREATIVITY IS...


"The tendency to generate or recognize ideas, alternatives, or possibilities that may be useful in solving problems, communicating with others, and entertaining ourselves and others."
